The Staff

Ted Lasso, portrayed by Jason Sudekis

Jason Sudeikis is an American actor known for his comedic talent. He gained popularity as a cast member on "Saturday Night Live" and showcased his wit through memorable character portrayals. Sudeikis transitioned to film with successful comedies like "Horrible Bosses" and "We're the Millers." His acclaimed series "Ted Lasso" won him a Golden Globe for his role as an optimistic football coach. Alongside his acting career, Sudeikis is involved in philanthropy. With his versatile performances and likable demeanor, he continues to captivate audiences, leaving a lasting impact on the entertainment industry.

Rebbeca Welton, portrayed by Hannah Waddingham

Hannah Waddingham is a talented British actress known for her captivating performances on stage and screen. With her commanding presence and powerful voice, she has made a remarkable impact in the entertainment industry. Waddingham's journey began in the theater, where she showcased her exceptional talent in productions like "Spamalot" and "The Wizard of Oz." She later transitioned to television, gaining widespread recognition for her role as Septa Unella in "Game of Thrones." Her portrayal of the formidable character earned her critical acclaim. With her versatility and dedication to her craft, Hannah Waddingham continues to shine as a standout performer.

Roy Kent, portrayed by Brett Goldstein

Brett Goldstein is a multi-talented British actor, writer, and comedian who has made a notable impact in the entertainment industry. Known for his sharp wit and versatile performances, Goldstein has garnered recognition both on screen and on stage. He has appeared in popular television shows such as "Derek" and "Drifters," showcasing his acting prowess. In addition, Goldstein is a skilled writer and stand-up comedian, delivering his unique brand of humor to audiences worldwide. More recently, he gained widespread acclaim for his role as Roy Kent in the hit series "Ted Lasso," which has further solidified his status as a rising star in the industry.

Coach Beard, portrayed by Brendan Hunt

Brendan Hunt is a talented American actor, writer, and improviser who has made a notable mark in the entertainment industry. Known for his versatility and comedic skills, Hunt has showcased his talent both on stage and screen. He gained widespread recognition for his role as Coach Beard in the hit series "Ted Lasso," where his deadpan humor and chemistry with co-stars earned him critical acclaim. In addition to acting, Hunt is also a skilled writer and has contributed to the success of "Ted Lasso" as a writer and producer. With his comedic timing and creative contributions, Brendan Hunt continues to leave a lasting impression on audiences.
